Leaning Chairs

The Chairs are designed simulate the feeling of balancing the chair on two legs while leaning back. The chair was designed for the chair design coures and is made out of pine wood. The chairs structure are made up of two main elements the seat and the frame. The frame remains solid while the seat can freely rotate 45 degrees. The chairs were designed to be intuitive allowing the user to easly control the degrees of leaning via how much you leaned your head backwards and forwards. This level of control was accomplised by verticaly aligning the axis of rotation with the spin
