Violen Shoulder Rest

The Violen Shoulder rest is deisgned to allow the use to more comforably use and control the violen while playing. this product was designed and manufactured for the industrial 3D printing course. the material is 3D printed plastic made up of three main parts the padding, the frame, and the violen holder.
