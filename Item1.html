Golf Cribbage Board

The Cribage board is designed in the format of a real golf coure (Moose Lake Golf Coure). The board is made out of birch plywood(1/8, 1/16) and consistsof multiple layors creating 3D effect. The material was cut out and engraved using a laser engraver based on my own designs
